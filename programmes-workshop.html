Pracademic Syllabus

Our “Practical Academic” Syllabus
are based on analytical research and real case studies.

  • Why Joining our Workshop?

    CECT (Center for Entrepreneurship, Change and Third Sector) Trisakti University as our research center, Sustainability consultant and think tank of MM-Sustainability Trisakti University expand it services to provide Sustainability Workshop Series.
    Since 2007, CECT has been working with more than 40 clients in the area of Manufacturing, Banking, Mining, Oil and Gas, Retail, Consulting, Agriculture, and many more. We apply and iterate the real case studies of our clients to the workshop classes as the materials, so that it enables participants to gain their problem-solving and system thinking skills.

    We aim to inspire industry professionals to articulate their skills and get more knowledge in order to achieve the Sustainable Future of their organization/business.

    We believe, this workshop will bring a transformational change to unlock your great potential. The workshop course is ranging from Sustainability, ISO 26000, Measuring Social Return of Sustainability Investment, Designing Sustainability Roadmap, Conflict Resolution & Negotiation Skills, Designing Community Development Program, Project Management, Risk Mitigation Strategy, and many more.

    Think Forward, Think the Future You.
