Why Choose
MM-Sustainability?

Established in 2008, MM-Sustainability (previously MM-CSR) aims to contribute to sustainable development through education with PRACADEMIC (Practical Academic) curriculum. 

  • Teaching Block System

    Applying "Teaching Block System" methodology that allows students to focus 1 (one) subject per month.

  • Experienced
    Academic Leaders

    Learn from the experienced
    academic leaders and mentors from industry professionals.

  • Weekend Class

    Great schedule for working professionals, classes will be conducted only on weekends,
    located in Sudirman area.

  • Applicable Learning

    Applicable learning that enables
    you to achieve your future career goals in Sustainability.
