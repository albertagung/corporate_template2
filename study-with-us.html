  • ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

    To be admitted into the MM-Sustainability Trisakti University Program, applicant must meet the admission requirements:

    1. Applicants must have completed an S1 or any other undergraduate degree program from an accredited higher education institution (with minimum accreditation: B).
    2. Applicants must complete and pass the MM-Sustainability Trisakti University scheduled written selection tests which includes an Academic Potential Test (TPA); Mathematics, Statistics, Introduction of Management and Economic Theories as well as an English test. On successful completion of the TPA candidates shall go an in-depth interview conducted by MM-Sustainability Trisakti University.

  • DOCUMENT REQUIREMENTS

    Applicant must provide:

    1. 2 (two) copies of S1 Certificate or undergraduate certificate and accompanying transcript that has been certified by the issuing faculty or university.
    2. 2 (two) recommendation letters, using the recommendation letter forms provided.
      Recommendation letters may be provided from any of the following:
      • Lecturers (provided they have no family relationship with applicant)
      • Current or previous supervisors (provided they have no family relationship with applicant)
    3. 2 (two) copies of an ID Card (KTP/SIM/Passport/KITAS)
    4. 6 (six) pcs passport-size coloured photos of each size- 3x4 and 2x3.
